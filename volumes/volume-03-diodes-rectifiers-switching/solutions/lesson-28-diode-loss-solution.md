# Lesson 28 Challenge — Worked Solution

Conduction power:

$$P_{cond}=0.9\text{ V}\cdot3\text{ A}\cdot0.4=1.08\text{ W}$$

Recovery-related energy:

$$E_{rr}\sim200\text{ V}\cdot120\text{ nC}=24\ \mu J$$

At 150 kHz:

$$P_{rr}\sim24\ \mu J\cdot150\text{ kHz}=3.6\text{ W}$$

The recovery term is larger than conduction loss in this estimate. Remaining terms include junction-capacitance energy, leakage, diode switching overlap, ringing/snubber loss, temperature dependence, and energy dissipated in the commutating switch rather than the diode itself.
