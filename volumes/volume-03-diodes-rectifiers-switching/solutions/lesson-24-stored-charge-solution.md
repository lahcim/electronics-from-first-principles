# Lesson 24 Challenge — Worked Solution

Stored charge estimate:

$$Q_S\approx I_F\tau_T=(2\text{ A})(50\text{ ns})=100\text{ nC}$$

If reverse current were a constant 4 A and recombination were ignored, the shortest charge-removal interval would be:

$$t\approx Q/I=100\text{ nC}/4\text{ A}=25\text{ ns}$$

A real waveform is not rectangular, and recombination removes part of the charge. Peak reverse current, current slope, temperature, and circuit inductance determine the observed recovery interval.
