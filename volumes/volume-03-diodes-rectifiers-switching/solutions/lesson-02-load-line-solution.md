# Lesson 2 Challenge — Worked Solution

Use the worst-case limits.

For maximum current at 10 V and $V_F=0.55$ V:

$$I_{max}=\frac{10-0.55}{R}$$

For minimum current at 8 V and $V_F=0.80$ V:

$$I_{min}=\frac{8-0.80}{R}$$

Choose 1.8 kΩ from E24.

$$I_{max}\approx5.25\text{ mA}$$

$$I_{min}=4.00\text{ mA}$$

Maximum resistor power:

$$P_{R,max}=I_{max}^2R\approx49.6\text{ mW}$$

A 0.125 W resistor is adequate electrically, though 0.25 W gives more thermal margin.
