# Lesson 8 KiCad 10 project

Open `lesson-08.kicad_pro`, then open `lesson-08.sch`. Let KiCad 10 import and save it as `lesson-08.kicad_sch`.

Run a DC operating-point analysis. No schematic SPICE directive is required.

Expected baseline:

- current magnitude: 2 mA;
- `V(VIN)` = 10 V;
- `V(N1)` = 8 V;
- `V(N2)` = 4 V;
- resistor drops: 2 V, 4 V, and 4 V.

Verify that all three resistor currents have the same magnitude.
